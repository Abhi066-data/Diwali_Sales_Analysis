# 📌 Overview

This project performs **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on retail sales data during the Diwali festival season to identify purchasing patterns, customer demographics, and product performance.

The goal is to generate **actionable business insights** that can help retailers optimize festive marketing strategies.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Festive seasons generate large spikes in retail sales. However, without data analysis, businesses struggle to understand:

• Customer purchasing patterns
• High-performing product categories
• Geographic revenue distribution

This project demonstrates how **data analytics can drive strategic business decisions.**

---

Customer demographics

# 📊 Key Insights

• Female customers contributed a higher share of purchases
• Certain states generated significantly higher revenue
• A small number of product categories dominated total sales
